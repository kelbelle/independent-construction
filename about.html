Building with Integrity in the Bozeman Area

Independent Construction is a family-owned general contracting company proudly serving the Greater Bozeman area since 2010. Founded by Clay Feliciano, we specialize in high-quality residential remodeling and renovations, turning homeowners' visions into reality one project at a time.

With deep roots in Montana, we understand the unique needs of homes in our mountain communities—from Bozeman's growing neighborhoods to the rugged beauty of Big Sky and the historic charm of Butte and Helena. Our team brings years of hands-on experience in kitchen, bathroom, bedroom, garage, laundry, and full-home remodels, always prioritizing craftsmanship, clear communication, and respect for your home.

What sets us apart? We're not just builders—we're your neighbors. We keep projects on time and on budget, use premium materials suited to Montana's climate, and treat every job like it's our own. Whether it's a complete kitchen transformation or a thoughtful garage conversion, we deliver results you can trust.

Thank you for considering Independent Construction. We're excited to partner with you and build something lasting.
